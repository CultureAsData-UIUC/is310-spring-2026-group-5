1. Tone is subjective and you may find that many media outlets talk about AI in schools in a negative light, but this is not a detriment to your project. It is fine that your dataset seems to have many sources with one-sided sentiment. If you have enough data points selected in a random way and this seeming asymmetry is still the case, it is likely that you are looking at the signal you want to find amid the noise using sentiment analysis. You should try to have a large enough dataset that there is a low risk of other forms of sampling bias, but you should not try to structure it to contain "balanced" data: this will skew the results of your analysis.
2. I would encourage you to explore reasons why you may be seeing differences in the way that AI in schools is discussed in the media. What stake do any of these journalists or institutions have in portraying these events the way they do? Are there any anomalies in your data that show some media sources having a particular bias?
