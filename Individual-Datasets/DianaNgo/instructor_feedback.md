# Instructor Feedback for Initial Dataset

Hi Diana,

I think narrowing the scope of your project to these two hashtags was a good choice. It looks like comparing the two is already yielding some interesting differences/similarities. Your dataset looks good for the most part, but I have a few alterations/suggestions:

1. There is a formatting error where your second column appears as "Sheet1!I1", which should be removed.
2. It is good that you are including the link to the video, but given the ephemeral nature of TikTok videos, I would suggest including some information about the content of the video itself. For example, you could include a caption, if it is available.
3. For the comment type columns, your categories are quite vague. Nearly every comment is an opinion or reaction. The ubiquity of the category means it is not the most helpful in understanding the comment. I would recommend adding a few more categories to this column.

In your documentation you write "wouldn't it make more sense that a video from #BookTok showing me the top books in 2026 has more saves than a video from #DanceTok with someone dancing to a trending song?" I am not sure I understand what you mean here. Do you mean that BookTok is algorithmically disadvantaged? Your research motivations need to be clearer. Why these two communities? What do we hope to learn by comparing the two?

It's good that you are noting the influence of the TikTok algorithm at play between the two communities, but why might that difference exist? BookTok has a greater barrier to entry: you need to acquire and read the books people are talking about if you want to converse with them about it. The dynamics of the community itself will also impact how popular they are.

Finally, you did not include a next steps section so it is unclear how you plan to scale this project up for the final submission. You cannot use TikTok's API, so how do you intend to add to this dataset? This is a requirement for the final project, so please do not overlook this step.

Please feel free to reach out to me if you have any questions.
